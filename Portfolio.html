Turning Data Into Business Decisions

I'm a fresh Business Information Systems graduate from Helwan University — GPA 4.0, ranked 7th in my class — with a deep passion for Data Analysis, Business Intelligence, and Business Analysis. I'm genuinely excited about extracting insights that drive real decisions, not just crunching numbers.

My path has been a mix of academic rigor and real-world experience: interning at IDSC on national-scale trade data, completing Egypt's DEPI data analytics scholarship, working inside PepsiCo's IT infrastructure, and sitting across from loan officers at Banque Misr and CIB learning how financial decisions actually get made. Right now, I'm focused on building data solutions that matter — clean pipelines, sharp BI dashboards, and business analysis that decision-makers can actually use. Whether it's SQL querying, Power BI storytelling, or Python-powered EDA, I turn data into clarity.

End-to-end data analysis project on Egypt's export data to evaluate sector performance, analyze trends over time, and segment countries based on export behavior.
